Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does UAE visa processing take?
Standard visit visa processing takes 3–5 working days after we receive all your documents. Urgent processing (1–2 days) is available at an additional fee. We'll keep you updated via WhatsApp throughout.
Do you accept M-Pesa for payment?
Yes! We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash. For UAE-based clients, we accept AED cash and local bank transfers. Payment details are shared on WhatsApp once we confirm your service.
What documents do I need for a UAE visit visa?
Typically: a valid passport (minimum 6 months validity), a recent passport-size photo, and your return flight ticket or itinerary. Some applicants may need a bank statement or proof of accommodation. We'll send you a personalised checklist after you contact us.
Are the job links legitimate and verified?
Yes. Every job listing we share is vetted through our network of trusted UAE employers and licensed recruitment partners. We do not charge recruitment fees from candidates — you only pay for visa and travel services if needed.
Do you offer airport pickup in Kenya too?
Currently our airport pickup service is based in Dubai and Sharjah. For Kenya arrivals, we can connect you with trusted transport partners at JKIA Nairobi and Mombasa Moi International Airport.
How do I track my visa application?
We send you WhatsApp updates at every stage — submission, processing, and approval. You'll never be left wondering what's happening. If you have questions between updates, just message us anytime.
What if my visa application is rejected?
While rejections are rare when documents are complete and correct, if it happens we will advise you on the reason and re-apply where possible. Government visa fees are non-refundable, but our service fee handling is discussed case by case.
Can you help someone already in the UAE extend their visa?
Yes. We assist with visit visa extensions, status changes, and overstay fine assessments. Contact us on WhatsApp with your current visa copy and we'll advise on the best course of action.
